It is I  
the mask repairman  
come, put your mask  
in the van  
for some, a fast task  
for others, a long past  
I'll help you escape  
with a bit of crape and tape  
maybe leather or plastic  
or plaster fantastic  
just a dollar  
or even a holler  
an honest review  
an aah! or an ooh!  
something new  
to spread my view  
come, give me your mask  
I'll make it anew.
